# Product Requirements Document - experiment

**Author:** Luca Lanziani
**Date:** 2026-02-11

## Executive Summary

### Vision

A peer-to-peer employee recognition platform that boosts morale and productivity at my company by making appreciation visible, instant, and rewarding.

### Product Differentiator

Recognition is free and always public; rewards are gated. Every nomination instantly posts a public shout-out to the company Slack channel and the web app recognition feed -- regardless of whether the manager approves a gift card reward. This separation ensures the morale-boosting moment of public recognition is never blocked by budget constraints or approval delays.
